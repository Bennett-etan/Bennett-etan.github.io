My volleyball career started in Rochester, NY where I joined Pace Bootlegger as a middle block. Throughout high school, I played in the middle and transitioned to left side as my skills progressed. I began playing beach tournaments in sophomore year and continued in New Jersey after enrolling at Stevens Institute of Technology where I played middle block. After practicing on a first league team in Germany, I returned to New Jersey to finish my final collegiate season. As I build for the next beach season, I will be coaching on the east coast as head coach for the Stevens Men's Club Volleyball Team.
